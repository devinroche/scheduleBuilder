Class registration doesn't have to be this hard.

Class registration is an incredible hassle for all students. We know that students are busy and don't want to waste time looking for classes, deciding what to take, and finding times to take them. That's why we made Schedule Builder. With Schedule Builder, students can easily have schedules built for them.

Schedule Builder started as a CS224 project and has now turned into a full web application. We hope to continue to add more schools and features to Schedule Builder.
